Telemore is an AI-powered productivity task tracker that provides users with a comprehensive view of their activities, task progress, and productivity patterns through intelligent insights and agentic assistance, eliminating the need for manual tracking.

Telemore helps individuals and teams boost productivity, manage tasks, and streamline workflows with minimal disruption to daily operations. Its conversational interface and agentic task execution provide actionable recommendations to improve performance while actively assisting with task completion.

Telemore includes end-to-end data encryption with industry-leading standards such as AES-256 and TLS 1.3, ensuring that all your information is secure during system operations. Your data is encrypted and protected against unauthorized access.

Telemore is designed with security at the forefront. All conversations with the AI assistant stay completely confidential and aren't logged or stored. You maintain complete ownership of all your information, and we never use your data to train our systems or share it with third parties.

Using Telemore, you can expect increased productivity, smoother workflows, and AI-driven insights into task completion, performance trends, and potential bottlenecks. Telemore helps you optimize your work through intelligent automation that prioritizes tasks, suggests execution strategies, and delivers personalized recommendations based on your unique work patterns.
