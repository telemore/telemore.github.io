Telemore is an AI employee productivity monitoring software that provides managers with a comprehensive view of employee activity, task progress, and productivity levels without the need for micromanagement.

Telemore helps businesses monitor employee productivity, manage tasks, and streamline workflows with minimal disruption to daily operations. Its AI-driven insights provide actionable recommendations to improve performance.

Telemore includes secure data storage and backup features, ensuring that no data is lost during monitoring or system malfunctions. Your data is encrypted and backed up regularly for safety.

Telemore is designed with security at the forefront. We use encryption and comply with industry-leading privacy protocols to safeguard your employee data while maintaining user privacy. We do not access or store your data beyond troubleshooting needs.

Using Telemore, you can expect increased employee productivity, smoother workflows, and data-driven insights into task completion, performance trends, and potential productivity bottlenecks. Telemore helps you optimize team management while providing transparency into daily activities.
