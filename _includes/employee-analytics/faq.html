Telemore is primarily an AI-powered productivity task tracker that helps individuals optimize their workflow through intelligent insights. As an optional add-on feature, it offers employee productivity monitoring capabilities that provide managers with visibility into team activity, task progress, and productivity levels without micromanagement.

Telemore's core strength is its AI-driven productivity assistance that helps individuals chat with their tasks and benefit from agentic task execution. For businesses that need it, Telemore also offers employee monitoring capabilities that help streamline workflows with minimal disruption while providing actionable recommendations to improve team performance.

Telemore includes secure data storage and backup features across both its core productivity platform and optional monitoring features, ensuring that no data is lost during system operations. Your productivity data and any team monitoring data are encrypted and backed up regularly for safety.

Telemore is designed with security at the forefront. We use end-to-end encryption and comply with industry-leading privacy protocols to safeguard your personal productivity data and, if you use the monitoring add-on, your team data. All conversations with the AI assistant stay completely confidential and aren't logged or stored beyond immediate service needs.

Using Telemore's core platform, you can expect increased personal productivity, smoother workflows, and AI-driven insights into your own task completion and performance trends. With the optional employee monitoring add-on, you gain additional transparency into team activities, helping you optimize team management and identify potential productivity bottlenecks across your organization.
